Scaredy Cat

A silly retake on the origin of the phrase, told through graphics for desktop, tablet and phone icons. This clumsy cat just wanted to play, but a bucket of orange paint and some yarn led to some scared friends.

Logo for my Personal Brand


‘Out-of-the-box’ is how I wanted to reimagine myself as a brand.

I used a crisp but creative 3d perspective of my initials, ‘H’ and ‘G,’ to form a subtractive composition.

The G is open to light and ideas, which it lets through and projects on the ground as a circular arrow to symbolize my cycle of growth.
